  What is College ProcureEase?

College ProcureEase is an online platform designed to streamline procurement processes at M.H. Saboo Siddik College of Engineering. It offers a user-friendly interface for managing the college store and facilitating procurement activities.

 Who can use College ProcureEase?

College ProcureEase is accessible to all members of the M.H. Saboo Siddik College of Engineering community, including faculty, staff, and students.

 What roles are available on College ProcureEase?

There are two roles available: Member of the college store and Educator (Teachers and Students). Each role has specific features and functionalities tailored to their needs.

 How do I access my personalized dashboard?

After logging in, you will be directed to your personalized dashboard, which serves as the central hub for all store-related activities.

 Can I submit requests for specific items?

Yes, students and teachers can submit requests for stationary items or library resources available in the college. These requests will be reviewed by the store incharge for approval and fulfillment.

 Is there a limit to the number of requests I can submit?

There is no limit to the number of requests you can submit on College ProcureEase. Feel free to submit requests as needed for your college-related activities.

 How long does it take for requests to be processed?

The processing time for requests may vary depending on factors such as request volume and approval requirements. We strive to process requests promptly and will notify you of any updates via email.

 Can I track the status of my submitted requests?

Yes, you can track the status of your submitted requests through your dashboard. You will receive notifications regarding the approval status and availability updates for your requests.

 How do I add new products to the system?

Adding new products is easy! Simply navigate to the "New Product" section on your dashboard and fill out the required information in the data entry form. Once submitted, the product will be added to the system.

 Is there a limit to the number of products I can add to the system?

There is no limit to the number of products you can add to the system. Feel free to add products as needed to manage store inventory effectively.

 Can I generate reports on store activities and expenses?

Yes, you can generate reports on store activities and expenses through the reporting section on your dashboard. Customize the report parameters to generate insights tailored to your needs.

 How often are department and lab registers updated?

Department and lab registers are updated automatically in real-time whenever new product data is submitted. This ensures that all records are accurate and up-to-date at all times.

 How secure is my data on College ProcureEase?

College ProcureEase takes data security seriously and employs robust measures to protect user data. Your information is encrypted and kept confidential at all times.

 What if I encounter technical issues or need assistance?

If you encounter any technical issues or need assistance, you can contact us via WhatsApp. We are here to help resolve any issues you may have.
